   > ✗ "ascolta" → "ascolto" — 3rd person (ascolta = he/she listens) instead of 1st person (ascolto = I listen). The subject is "io" throughout: "Mentre studio, ascolto la musica."

7. _(audio)_

   > ✗ "le uve" → "l'uva" — uva is a collective singular noun (like "fruit" in English). You buy "l'uva" (the grapes), not "le uve". This is the uva/uova confusion from last time — by ear they sound similar, but uva = grapes (singular), uova = eggs (plural).
