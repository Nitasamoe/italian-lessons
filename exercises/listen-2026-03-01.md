## Italian Listening
Listen to each sentence and write what you hear.

1. _(audio)_
   > Le uove sono sul tavolo della cucina
   > ✗ "uove" → "uova" — spelling: the plural of uovo is uova (irregular). Rest is perfect.

2. _(audio)_
   > Qualche volta mangio le pizze con gli amichi?
   > ✗ "le pizze" → "la pizza", "amichi" → "amici" — Two errors: (1) it's singular "la pizza" here, (2) the plural of amico is amici (not amichi). Also not a question — no question mark needed.

3. _(audio)_
   > Andiamo al supermercato insieme dopo il pranzo
   > ✗ "dopo il pranzo" → "dopo pranzo" — no article after "dopo" with meals. Close though!

4. _(audio)_
   > non mi sento molto bene oggi
   > ✓ Correct. (Capitalization ignored for listening exercises.)

5. _(audio)_
   > compro delle patate e delle carote
   > ✓ Correct.

6. _(audio)_
   > Lei parla rapidamente in italiano
   > ✓ Correct.

7. _(audio)_
   > non beviamo quasi mai il vino rosso
   > ✓ Correct.

8. _(audio)_
   > i bambini vanno a scuola ogni mattina
   > ✓ Correct.

9. _(audio)_
   > voglio del'uova e della arancia per favore
   > ✗ "del'uova" → "dell'uva", "della arancia" → "delle arance" — (1) uva (grapes) not uova (eggs) — key homophone pair! (2) dell' before vowels needs double l, (3) "delle arance" is plural. Note: uva/uova sound very similar — good ear, wrong word.

10. _(audio)_
    > Ci alziamo presto alla mattina
    > ✗ Missing "sempre", "alla mattina" → "la mattina" — (1) the sentence includes "sempre" before "presto", (2) use "la mattina" not "alla mattina".

## Summary
5/10 correct. Strong on verb conjugations and basic sentence structure — sentences 4–8 were all perfect. Main issues: (1) uva vs uova confusion struck again (#1 and #9), (2) article usage after prepositions (#3, #10), (3) spelling of plurals like amici (#2). The uva/uova pair is especially tricky by ear — worth drilling.
