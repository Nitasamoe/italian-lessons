# Drill — 2026-03-01c — Spelling (Double Consonants, Accents & Common Words)

Focus: Your most frequent error type (24 total). Translate each short German sentence into Italian — pay close attention to spelling!

1. Meine Schwester trinkt Milch.
   > La mia sorella beve latte.
   > ✓ Correct! "Sorella" spelled right. Note: "il latte" (with article) is more standard, but acceptable without.

2. Ich möchte einen Kaffee ohne Zucker.
   > Vorrei und caffè senza zucchero
   > ✗ "und" → "un" — you wrote the German word "und" (and) instead of Italian "un" (a/an). "Zucchero" is correct though!

3. Die Großmutter kocht Steaks.
   > La nonna cucina bistecche.
   > ✓ Correct! "Bistecche" with double c — nailed it!

4. Wir gehen nach Hause.
   > Andiamo a casa.
   > ✓ Correct! "Casa" — not "case" this time.

5. Die Stadt ist schön.
   > La citta è bella.
   > ✗ "citta" → "città" — the accent is required. This is the same error from the exercise. città, città, città!

6. Meine Freundin ist sehr nett.
   > La mia amica è buona.
   > ✗ "Amica" spelled correctly — good! But missing "molto" for "sehr" and "buona" (good) isn't quite "nett" — better: "La mia amica è molto gentile/simpatica."

7. Die Karotten sind frisch.
   > Le carote sono fresce.
   > ✗ "fresce" → "fresche" — needs an h! Italian spelling rule: fresco → fresche (fem. plural). The "sch" sound before e/i is spelled "sche/schi".

8. Die Kinder schlafen in ihrem Zimmer.
   > I bambini dormono nel loro camera.
   > ✗ "nel" → "nella" — camera is feminine, so in + la = nella (not nel = in + il). The rest is perfect.

9. Vor dem Essen wasche ich mir die Hände.
   > Prima il pranzo mi lavo le mane.
   > ✗ Two errors: "Prima il" → "Prima del" — prima di + il = prima del (needs the "di"). "mane" → "mani" — le mani (irregular feminine plural). Good news: "prima" not "primo"!

10. Ich bin um sieben Uhr aufgestanden.
    > Mi sono alzato alle sette.
    > ✓ Perfect! "Alzato" (not svegliato), "mi sono" (correct auxiliary), "alle sette" — all correct.

## Summary
4/10 correct. The target words from past errors are improving: sorella ✓, bistecche ✓, casa ✓, zucchero ✓, amica ✓ — that's 5/5 previously misspelled words now correct! But new spelling issues appeared (fresce→fresche, mane→mani) and città still missing its accent. The nel/nella gender agreement error in #8 is a grammar issue, not just spelling.
